Move past static pages into **data**. You will model information in MySQL and NoSQL stores, expose it through APIs, and build views that answer real questions.

Your capstone is a **data dashboard** — live charts and tables fed by your own backend. Along the way you use AI to explore schemas, draft queries, and sketch chart layouts, then verify every result against the real database.
