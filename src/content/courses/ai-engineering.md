Every Rogue course builds AI literacy in context. This one is for builders who want to **engineer AI systems** — not just prompt a chat window.

You will work with model APIs, retrieval patterns, agents/tools, evaluation harnesses, cost and latency trade-offs, and safety/guardrails suitable for something real users touch. Capstone: ship an AI-backed feature or agent with measurable quality bars and documented failure modes — something you would defend in a design review.
